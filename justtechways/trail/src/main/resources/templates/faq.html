Digital marketing encompasses all marketing efforts that use electronic devices or the internet. It includes various online channels such as search engines, social media, email, websites, and mobile apps to connect with current and prospective customers. It often involves strategies like search engine optimization (SEO), content marketing, social media marketing, email marketing, and paid advertising.
